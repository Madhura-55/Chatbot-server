# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Login
**Q: How do I reset my password?**
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email to receive a password reset link valid for 30 minutes.

**Q: Can I have multiple addresses saved?**
Yes, you can save multiple shipping addresses under "Account Settings" > "Addresses" and select one at checkout.

## Payments
**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, popular wallets, and Cash on Delivery (where eligible).

**Q: Is it safe to save my card details?**
Card details are tokenized and stored securely through our PCI-DSS compliant payment partner; we do not store raw card numbers.

**Q: My payment was deducted but the order shows as failed. What now?**
If an amount is deducted but the order isn't confirmed, it is automatically reversed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. No action is needed on your part.

## Orders
**Q: Can I cancel my order after placing it?**
Orders can be cancelled free of charge as long as they haven't been shipped. Once shipped, cancellation is not possible, but you may initiate a return after delivery.

**Q: How do I change the quantity or variant of an item after ordering?**
Order details cannot be modified after placement. Please cancel the order (if not yet shipped) and place a new one with the correct details.

## Sellers & Marketplace
**Q: Who do I contact if a seller is unresponsive?**
All orders, payments, and returns are handled centrally through the platform, not directly with sellers. Use "My Orders" > "Get Help" for any seller-related issue, and our support team will mediate.

## Loyalty & Discounts
**Q: How do coupon codes work?**
Coupon codes can be applied at checkout in the "Apply Coupon" field. Only one coupon can be applied per order, and coupons cannot be combined with certain ongoing sale discounts.
