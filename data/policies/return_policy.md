# Return & Refund Policy

## Eligibility
Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by the invoice. Perishable goods, personal care items, and items marked "non-returnable" on the product page cannot be returned.

## Process
1. Go to "My Orders" in your account dashboard.
2. Select the order and click "Request Return".
3. Choose a reason and submit the request.
4. A pickup will be scheduled within 2-3 business days.

## Refunds
Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after the returned item passes quality inspection. For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are issued as bank transfers; customers will be asked to provide bank details.

## Damaged or Wrong Items
If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, contact support within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the product and packaging. These cases are eligible for free replacement or full refund regardless of the standard return window.

## Non-Returnable Categories
- Innerwear and swimwear
- Cosmetics and personal care products once opened
- Perishable food items
- Customized or personalized products
- Gift cards and digital vouchers
