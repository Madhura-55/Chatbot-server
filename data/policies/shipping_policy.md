# Shipping & Delivery Policy

## Delivery Timelines
- Standard delivery: 3-7 business days depending on location.
- Express delivery (where available): 1-2 business days for an additional fee.
- Remote or rural areas may experience delays of 1-3 extra days.

## Shipping Charges
- Orders above ₹499 qualify for free standard shipping.
- Orders below ₹499 incur a flat shipping fee of ₹49.
- Express shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on weight and distance.

## Order Tracking
Once an order is shipped, a tracking number is generated and can be viewed under "My Orders" > "Track Order". Tracking status updates may take up to 24 hours to reflect after dispatch.

## Failed Delivery Attempts
If a delivery attempt fails due to the customer being unavailable, the courier will attempt delivery up to 2 more times over the next 2-3 days. After 3 failed attempts, the order is returned to the seller and a refund is initiated (for prepaid orders) or the order is cancelled (for Cash on Delivery).

## Cash on Delivery (COD)
COD is available for orders up to ₹5,000. A small COD handling fee of ₹20 may apply. COD is not available for certain high-value or fragile categories.

## Address Changes
Delivery address can be changed only before the order is shipped. Once shipped, the address cannot be modified, but you may contact support to redirect the package where possible.
